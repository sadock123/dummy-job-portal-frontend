Job Title: KITES Intern
Company: Imperial Consumer Brands
Location: Pan-India
Experience Required: MBA Intern
Stipend: ₹ 2,20,000 per month

Job Description:
Knowledge, Initiative & Talent Excellence Series (KITES) is Imperial Consumer Brands' flagship Summer Internship Programme designed to provide leaders of tomorrow with a unique platform to learn from, experience and manage real business situations. KITES is a program where students have the opportunity to experience the rigor of live business scenarios, apply their theoretical learnings and gain precious insights into management, leadership and navigating dynamic challenges.

Internships are offered for two months each year. KITES is a critical platform that enables us to identify exceptional talent from the best institutes in India who have the potential to become the next generation of leaders. Based on their performance during the internship, interns are awarded Pre-Placement Offers of employment.

Key Skills Required:

Leadership & Team Management

Business Strategy formulation

Cross-functional Problem Solving

Stakeholder Communication
